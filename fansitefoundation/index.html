Chris Evans is an American actor. His acting career began with roles in TV shows such as Opposite Sex, and teen movies like Not Another Teen Movie. He gained notable attention for his portrayl of Human Torch in Marvel Comic's Fantastic Four, but is most known for playing the role of Captain America/Steve Rogers in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. In addition to acting, Evans produced, directed and starred in the 2014 movie Before We Go, and made his Broadway debut in 2018 in Lobby Hero.(Information courtesy of Wikipedia)

Chris Evans as Captain America

Evans has played Captain America in the Marvel Cinematic Universe since "Captain America: The First Avenger" in 2011. He reprised the role in eight Marvel movies total. Within the franchise, his character his own triology, and he appeared in the four "Avengers" movies. Captain America also has a cameo role "Spiderman Homecoming" from 2016. Fun Facts About Chris

  • He was born June 13, 1981 in Boston, Massachusetts
  • He got into acting after an interest in musical theatre, and attended acting camp as a child
  • He has one brother and two sisters
    • Carly Evans
    • Shanna Evans
    • Scott Evans
  • He never attended college, but took classes at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute
  • He was raised Catholic but now considers himself a student of Buddhism
